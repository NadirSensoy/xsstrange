Xsstrange

Welcome to Xsstrange! Developed by the Gallipoli Community, our project is a open source platform designed to simulate realistic web vulnerabilities in a safe, legal, and collaborative environment. Inspired by the valor and strategic ingenuity of the historic Gallipoli campaign, our community is committed to forging a digital proving ground where cybersecurity skills are honed and innovations in defense are born.

  • Realistic Scenarios: Our platform meticulously simulates modern web vulnerabilities - such as XSS, SSRF, and CORS -in a controlled, authentic environment.
  • Open Source Collaboration: Built for transparency and innovation, Xsstrange invites community contributions and offers a secure, legal space to experiment with cutting-edge security tools.
  • Modular and Scalable: With Docker-powered containerization, create and customize your testing environment with ease, adapting to evolving security challenges.

Getting Started

Ready to explore the world of web security? Here's how to get started:

  1. Clone the Xsstrange repository from GitHub.
  2. Run the Docker Compose command to build the environment.
  3. Access the Xsstrange platform in your browser.
